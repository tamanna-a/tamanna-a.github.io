About Me

Hello I'm Tamanna!

Hello I am Tamanna! I am a masters student in Computer Science at Columbia University, specializing in Machine Learning.

I graduated from Princeton University and I worked most recently as a Data Scientist at Rolex USA, where I was entrusted to lead the US team on a global project to improve the watch repair process. In that role, I came across decades of valuable data that just wasn’t being utilized because it was free form text. I wanted to parse this data, which led me to Natrual Language Processing and voila, the rest is history!

I love all things languages. I currently do research in conversational AI systems. I was a Teaching Assistant for Columbia’s Natural Language Processing course taught By Yassine Benajiba in Fall 2021. In my free time, I am teaching myself French.
